Creative Mode


Living in the world of ideas and of dreams coming true and becoming part of our material world I've realized that is common to perceive two clear things; projections and reflections.

A projection comes from a source and a reflection is a projection that is 'not' connected to the source anymore but it bounces around as a constant that gets distorted. Ex. DBC under the vision of its founder versus other Code School that just tries to follow the trend.

Depending on the source the projection can be better, more clear and sustainable; in time and space than the reflection. The reflection is not more that a constant, it bounces around without much sense, but sometimes that reflection feeds the creativity of an other source and is in these cases that we have again a new projection. This can be DBC model inspiring an other Code School founder or members to have a new vision and dream and project that.

DBC is the projection of a dream, of a clear vision. Where we are all invited to project it, not just reflect it and in order to do that each one of us have to become a creative source.

Being a creative source is not a passive roll, is not consuming and throwing away. It is completely the opposite, it's and active roll. It comes with a huge responsibility, the better my projection the better DBC's and vise versa. And it is why I want to become a developer.

What part of the essence of DBC I'm going to be able to internalize, and make it part of me in order to carry it around as a torch? I have no idea but I'll keep that intuitive process to that part of me that have nothing to do with words and languages. But I'll do my best to co-create the experience and became a contrubution to it.

DBC was the last Code School I applied, I knew about these schools throw a person in North Carolina (DBC was not in the list)... and it was not until I started reading Quora that DBC pop to my top of mind.

The name kind of rejected me because the warlike concept behind, but the video on the web was the key. I just want to make the point that the conviction of DBC's founder, his idea and the way he us inviting us to project it was all I need it to feel.

I can't be more exited to be part of this experiment. I've met great people so far and I have had an incredible time.

Now it is time to make this next 9 weeks, my next 9 weeks. Own them and share them. Plus don't just like them but love them.
